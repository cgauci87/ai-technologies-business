AI & ML have been playing a critical role in medical industry. From disease identification and diagnosis, helping identify patients for clinical trials to drug manufacturing and predictive forecasting, these technologies have proven critical. State-of-the-art algorithms have achieved accuracies which are at par with human experts in the field of medical sciences.

"It takes an average of 12 years for a drug to travel from the research lab to the patient. By directing the latest advances in AI to streamline the drug discovery and drug repurposing processes there is the potential to significantly cut both the time to market for new drugs and their costs."
"A high proportion of mammograms yield false results, leading to 1 in 2 healthy women being told they have cancer. The use of AI is enabling review and translation of mammograms 30 times faster with 99% accuracy, reducing the need for unnecessary biopsies"
"Robots help doctors perform complex procedures with a precision, flexibility and control that goes beyond human capabilities. Robot-assisted surgeries have led to fewer surgery-related complications, less pain and a quicker recovery time."

Instances of AI uses within the Medical Industry

Research & Development: AI would enable R&D to scrap the traditional trial methods and embrace a more patient-driven biology via using more data-derived predictive hypotheses.

Precision Medicine:AI apps helps in tailoring of medical treatment to the individual characteristics of each patient.

Clinical Diagnosis: The use of AI can contribute in early detection of diseases including cancer. The process of Analyses and review of mammogram, radiology images can be speed up to 30 times with 99% accuracy using AI.

AI-assisted robotic surgery: AI can help improve surgical performance. Utilization of AI can reduce the human variations and improve the efficiency.

Artificial intelligence is fast evolving. Banking and finance industry are one of those finding ways to leverage this game changing technology. Many financial institutions use AI and ML applications to streamline their operations such as automation process to identify fraud , detecting unusual transactions. AI solutions can also assist in making the process of credit risk analysis more efficient, accurate, and effective. Front and middle-office AI applications offer the greatest cost savings opportunity across digital banking. AI in the realm of finance powers many solutions aimed at enhancing security precautions. As AI continues to be a growing force within fintech, experts believe its usage will spread across more sectors.

"70% of all financial services firms are using machine learning to predict cash flow events, fine-tune credit scores and detect fraud."

Instances of AI uses within the Finance Industry

Risk Management: Financial institutions integrate AI and ML applications to their existing systems so to assist analysts pinpoint trends and ensure better future planning.

Fraud Detection: Fraud detections solutions can capture abnormal transaction activity through specific algorithms.

Trading: AI can handle large data sets, analyse patterns and sift through data faster than humans, which expedites the entire process and saves large chunks of time.

Cyberattacks Prevention: Artificial intelligence can boost an organisation security by analyzing and determining normal data patterns and trends, and alerting companies of discrepancies or unusual activity.

AI is extensively being used in the manufacturing sector, facilitating the industrial automation. Artificial intelligence can ingest a combination of data from sensors, machines, and people and then apply it to algorithms designed to optimize operations or achieve lights out manufacturing. Process plants rely on AI to integrate data, analyze it, and produce the deep insights and predictions that help drive better decision-making across the board. AI-driven machines yields a bunch of benefits offering new opportunities, enhancing production efficiencies, and bringing machine interaction closer to human interaction. Many firms have already embraced AI technology to reduce downtime, and deliver high-quality products that meet unique consumer demands.

"Around 90% of organizations using these predictive analytics have seen an increased return on their investment. For 40% of those organizations that increase went up to over 10%, and sometimes even way more."
"93 percent of companies believe AI will be a pivotal technology to drive growth and innovation in the sector. China's performance in AI adoption is outstanding. The market size of AI in the manufacturing sector is expected to exceed USD2 billion by 2025, posting average annual growth of more than 40 percent from 2019. The rise of AI adoption in China's manufacturing sector has been boosted by favorable policies, ample funds, and the potential for AI implementation."

Instances of AI uses within the Manufacturing Industry

Quality Assessments: AI can identify minor faults in machinery or product, giving engineers the option to replicate the issue before it could become a major flaw.

Predictive Maintenance: ML-powered predictive tools and AI solutions can predict when equipment needs periodic maintenance so to avoid disruption that may occur on an entire production line.

Demand and Price Forecasting: With the use of collecting data, AI systems produce predictive analysis and combine it with human intelligence to forecast the demand for products and pricing efficiently.

Inventory Management: Machine Learning plays an integral role to manage and simplify management of inventories based on demand and supply which can reduce significant cost overheads for a manufacturing firm.
