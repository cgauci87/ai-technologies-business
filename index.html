What is AI?

AI technologies mimics human behavior in some way.

Think of it as a simulation of human intelligence processes by machines.


AI capabilities?

AI can make the user experience similar to interacting with a human.

Interpret external data.

To learn from such data.

To use such learnings to achieve specific goals and tasks through flexible adaptation.


How?

The input is huge amounts of data.

The human part is the output.

That’s what allows the AI to learn and adapt.

If it encounters a problem, it learns from the situation and recognizes a pattern.


AI in today's business landscape?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) were buzzwords a few years back.

Until some industries started levering the full potential and embraced such technologies.

Nowadays most businesses rely heavily on AI & ML applications and take advantage of the benefits.

Industries

Banking & Finance

Artificial intelligence is fast evolving. Banking and finance industry are one of those finding ways to leverage this game changing technology.

Many financial institutions use AI and ML applications to streamline their operations such as automation process to identify fraud , detecting unusual transactions.

AI solutions can also assist in making the process of credit risk analysis more efficient, accurate, and effective.

Front and middle-office AI applications offer the greatest cost savings opportunity across digital banking.

AI in the realm of finance powers many solutions aimed at enhancing security precautions.

As AI continues to be a growing force within fintech, experts believe its usage will spread across more sectors.

Manufacturing

AI is extensively being used in the manufacturing sector, facilitating the industrial automation.

Artificial intelligence can ingest a combination of data from sensors, machines, and people and then apply it to algorithms designed to optimize operations or achieve lights out manufacturing.

Process plants rely on AI to integrate data, analyze it, and produce the deep insights and predictions that help drive better decision-making across the board.

AI-driven machines yields a bunch of benefits – offering new opportunities, enhancing production efficiencies, and bringing machine interaction closer to human interaction.

Many firms have already embraced AI technology to reduce downtime, and deliver high-quality products that meet unique consumer demands.
